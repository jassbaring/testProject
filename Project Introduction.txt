                                 PLAN EASY!  LIVE EASY!

Our project is on Reminding and Task Scheduling. Now-a-days life is quite busy and 
everyone have to remember each and everything, which is not possible without the 
help of technology.Our main focus is to make people punctual and step towards their 
goal with accuracy. If you are bad in time management; this app will help you to 
schedule your task and give you reminders before the task that will inspire you with 
some motivational thoughts so that you can live your life easily.
